AKFA University was established in accordance with the resolution of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated February 15, 2019.

The main goal of our university is to become a leading higher educational institution in the region and to contribute to the development of Uzbekistan.

The purpose of our university: * Raise the level of education in the necessary sectors of the economy; * To offer solutions for the training of specialists; * Develop new educational technologies; * Develop the principles of education and training;

AKFA University offers 200 students from different countries training according to international standards.
